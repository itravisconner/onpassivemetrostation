Travel Times from Onpassive Metro Station

To Dubai Mall

  • Travel to Dubai Mall is typically via the metro Red Line heading towards UAE Exchange.

  • The nearest metro stop to the mall is Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall Station, just 5 stops and about 15-20 minutes from Onpassive Station.

  • From the station, a pedestrian walkway (air-conditioned) links directly to the mall, making this an easy and comfortable trip.

To Dubai International Airport

  • Dubai Airport has two key metro stations: Terminal 1 and Terminal 3, both on the Red Line.

  • Onpassive Station is approximately 11-13 stops away, with a travel time of roughly 30-40 minutes.

  • This journey offers a stress-free way to reach the airport without worrying about traffic or parking.

To Downtown Dubai

  • Downtown Dubai includes landmarks like the Dubai Opera and Dubai Fountain.

  • It's about 5-6 stops and 15 minutes by metro from Onpassive Station.

  • The closest metro stop for Downtown Dubai sightseeing is also Burj Khalifa/Dubai Mall Station.

Other Key Destinations

  • Business Bay: 3 stops, around 10 minutes

  • Mall of the Emirates: Approximately 9 stops and 25 minutes away via Al Barsha Station.

  • Expo 2020: About 25 stops and 50-60 minutes, ideal for special events or exhibitions.

Planning Your Journey from Onpassive Metro Station

  • Nol Card Usage: Make sure you have a Nol Card for easy and cashless payment across the metro system and buses.

  • Use Transit Apps: Dubai's RTA S'hail app allows you to check real-time train schedules and plan trips efficiently.

  • Consider Time of Day: Peak hours can affect travel times, especially mornings 7-9 AM and evenings 5-7 PM. Plan accordingly.

  • Combine Modes: For locations not directly served by metro, consider feeder buses or quick taxi rides from nearby metro stops.

  • Interlink with Nearby Metro Stations: Combine your journey with connections via stations like etisalat metro station dubai to reach wider destinations across the city.
